Redefining the Science of Silence

Features

Lose The Stress

We're constantly surrounded by noise. Be it a train station, a noisy coworker, or your lovely children. But in a world where we're becoming increasingly closer, we believe it should be easier to distance yourself a bit. Hush makes that a reality. With the press of a button, easily tune out into an aura of peace

Be Productive

It's no secret that more noise leads to less focus. But by how much? Some figures show the ability to focus can be increased by over 50%. With Hush, that far fetched figure has become more achievable than ever before with state of the art noise cancellation packaged right into a cross-platform mobile app

Universally Awesome

Any headphones, anywhere, at any time. All you need is a Hush account, and any pair of headphones. Additionally, Hush also works with both wireless and wired headphones, in ear or over-ear. So no matter the style, Hush has you covered with state of the art noise cancellation

Our Vision

At Hush we are in a constant endeavour is to bring the luxury of high quality noise canceling technology down to the average consumer. Ambient noise is far too ubiquitous in daily lives, rapidly invading the privacy of our dorms and home-offices, especially with urban sprawl continuing to threaten the solace of our homes. Coping with such noise can is an endeavour in itself, virtually impossible while undertaking tasks requiring deliberation. Hush’s diligent market research has demonstrated that 82% of consumers believe their methodology to fight ambient noise is flawed, yet 90% of participants would not cross the three digit threshold to purchase the growing necessity of headphones. At Hush we are engineering the ideal solution to this problem, which is with the use of active noise canceling technology at low prices. To buy headphones with proper active noise cancelling the price tag is an insulting 300 dollars, 300% of what the average consumer is willing to indulge for a pair of headphones. At Hush we aim to provide this sought-after, yet high end technology at a fraction of the price, through diligent and groundbreaking engineering.
